About me

Hi, my name is Navaneetha Krishnan Kondu Reddi a graduate from University of Toronto. I hold a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Chemical Engineering and an Emphasis in Data Analytics. Since I was young, I have always enjoyed to solve puzzles. So that’s how I look at big data sets: to me it is one big puzzle I want to solve. Finding patterns nobody else would see has always been a challenge to me.

Being a data enthusiast, over the past two years I have taken immense efforts to cement my expertise in the fields of Business Intelligence, Data Analytics and Machine Learning. Having experience in analytic tools such as Power BI and Tableau, I believe that the essence of effective data analysis lies in the proper presentation and reporting of data. I have considerable experience in using for data visualization, dashboard creation, time series analysis and forecasting. I am proficient in the use of SQL as a query language to extract meaningful insights from data. I am competent in the use of AWS for ETL and data Integration, data warehouses using Redshift and schedulers such as Apache Airflow.
